About & privacy

Your microphone is always on while listening is enabled, but everything you say stays inside your browser. Chrome's local Web Speech recognizer scans for any of your agent trigger words (currently bro) without sending any audio to our server.

Only after an agent trigger word fires, the next ~3 seconds of speech are recorded with your microphone, uploaded to ElevenLabs Scribe for high-quality transcription, and the resulting text is sent to Browser Use Cloud as the task. Nothing else leaves your device.
